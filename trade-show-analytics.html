The Problem with Trade Show Analytics Today

Trade show organizers have always struggled to prove value to exhibitors. The standard metrics — total attendance, badge scans, and social media impressions — tell sponsors almost nothing about whether their booth investment was worthwhile. Exhibitors know it, which is why trade show rebooking rates often disappoint.

Evential's trade show analytics platform solves this with verified, real-time booth engagement data. Instead of estimating how many people walked past a booth, Evential tracks exactly how many attendees stopped, how long they stayed, whether they returned, and which ones consented to lead sharing. That's the trade show analytics data exhibitors have always wanted but never had access to.

For organizers, this transforms trade show analytics from a post-event exercise into a live operational tool — and converts sponsor reporting from a manual burden into an automated revenue-driving asset. Explore how sponsor ROI tracking works within Evential's trade show analytics platform.

How Evential Trade Show Analytics Works

Before your trade show, Evential ships BLE smart badges and gateway sensors to your venue. Your team distributes badges at registration; gateways install throughout the show floor. Once the show opens, Evential's trade show analytics platform begins capturing data automatically — no configuration changes needed mid-show.

Throughout the trade show, your organizer dashboard shows live check-in rates, booth visitor counts, session attendance, and real-time alerts when thresholds are reached. Automations handle routine tasks: notifying organizers when booths hit milestones, pushing data to your CRM via webhook, and triggering emails when conditions are met.

After your trade show closes, Evential auto-generates exhibitor ROI reports and compiles lead packages for each booth — ready to deliver to sponsors within 24 hours. The entire post-show reporting process that used to take days of manual work happens automatically.
