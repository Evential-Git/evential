What Does Conference Analytics Software Do?

Conference analytics software tracks the data your B2B conference generates in real time — attendee check-ins, session attendance, booth traffic, and sponsor engagement — and makes it immediately available in an organizer dashboard. Rather than collecting feedback forms after the fact, conference analytics software gives you live visibility into what's happening across your event venue as the conference unfolds.

Evential's conference analytics platform uses BLE smart badges and fixed gateway sensors to passively track attendee movement throughout your venue. The system captures which sessions drew the biggest crowds, which sponsor booths had the highest dwell time, and which attendees showed the most buying intent — all without any manual effort from your team or any behavior change required from attendees.

The conference analytics data feeds into automated sponsor ROI reports, a real-time organizer dashboard, and an event lead marketplace that lets you monetize attendee engagement data post-conference. See how it works in the full platform walkthrough.
