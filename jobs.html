About Evential

What you'd be building.

Founded in late 2025, Evential is an event analytics platform powered by BLE badge hardware and real-time software for B2B conference and trade show organizers. We give organizers live attendee tracking, automated sponsor ROI reports, and a qualified event lead generation marketplace — replacing manual spreadsheets with live, verified event intelligence.

Our first live deployment at the INtercollegiate Entrepreneurship Summit captured 1,485 data points per badge over 7.5 hours, identified a 39% no-show rate, and found $507 in preventable food waste. We're now scaling to paid pilot deployments. The founding engineers who join now will shape the product, culture, and company that emerges.

The mission is simple: make events effortless to run so that bringing people together is no longer difficult or expensive to measure.
