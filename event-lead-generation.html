What Makes Evential's Event Lead Generation Different?

Traditional event lead generation relies on booth staff manually scanning badges — a process that's inconsistent, effort-heavy, and misses the majority of interested attendees who walk by without stopping. The contacts you collect this way are often cold: people who happened to scan at the wrong moment, not people who showed genuine buying intent.

Evential's event lead generation works differently. Because Evential's BLE badge tracking system monitors every attendee's movement throughout the event, it captures not just who visited a booth, but how long they stayed, whether they returned, and what their engagement pattern looked like across the whole event. This behavioral data is far more predictive of purchase intent than a manual badge scan.

Qualified leads in Evential are defined by three criteria: the attendee visited the sponsor's booth, spent 5 or more minutes there (indicating genuine interest rather than a passing glance), and consented to data sharing at event check-in. This qualification standard produces lead packages sponsors can actually act on — not just a list of names.

How Event Lead Generation Works in Evential

01

Attendee Consents at Check-In

During event registration and check-in, attendees are presented with a clear opt-out to share their contact information with exhibitors whose booths they engage with. No consent, no PII — movement data remains anonymous.

02

Evential Tracks Booth Engagement

BLE badge sensors passively track every attendee who enters a sponsor's booth zone — capturing visit duration, return visits, and time of day. This data is collected for all attendees, whether or not they consented.

03

Qualified Leads Are Identified

At event close, Evential's event lead generation platform identifies attendees who: (1) visited the sponsor's booth, (2) spent 5+ minutes there, and (3) consented to contact sharing. This three-part filter produces genuinely qualified leads.

04

Lead Packages Are Compiled Automatically

Evential automatically compiles each sponsor's qualified leads into a formatted lead report — name, contact info, visit duration, return visit data — ready for the organizer to review and deliver.

05

Organizers Sell Leads Through the Marketplace

Organizers sell lead packages directly to sponsors through Evential's built-in event lead marketplace — creating post-event revenue that didn't exist before. Sponsors receive verified, immediately actionable contacts.
